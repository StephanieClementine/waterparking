Water Use Restriction Information


Level One Restrictions


  • No person shall sprinkle or allow sprinkling except at premises: with even numbered civic addresses, on Thursdays and Sundays between the hours of 4:00 a.m. and 9:00 a.m. and between the hours of 7:00 p.m. and 10:00 p.m.; and
  • No person shall sprinkle or allow sprinkling except at premises: with odd numbered civic addresses, or with no civic address, on Wednesdays and Saturdays between the hours of 4:00 a.m. and 9:00 a.m. and between the hours of 7:00p.m. and 10:00 p.m

Level Two Restrictions


  • with even numbered civic addresses, on Thursdays between the hours of 4:00 a.m. and 9:00 a.m. and between the hours of 7:00 p.m. and 10:00 p.m.; and
  • with odd numbered civic addresses, or with no civic address, on Wednesdays between the hours of 4:00 a.m. and 9:00 a.m. and between the hours of 7:00p.m. and 10:00 p.m.
  • use a garden hose to wash sidewalks, driveways, roofs or other outdoor surfaces
  • use a garden hose to wash motor vehicles unless the hose is equipped with a shut off device that is spring loaded and operated by hand pressure.

Level Three Restrictions


  • sprinkle or allow sprinkling at any time;
  • use a garden hose to wash sidewalks, driveways, roofs or other outdoor surfaces
  • use a garden hose to wash motor vehicles unless the hose is equipped with a shut off device that is spring loaded and operated by hand pressure.
